The rain forest of the Amazon region is the home of about 220 thousand people from about 180 different tribes who live deep in the forest .The rainforest itself is an important environment but ,because of its size and location ,It also play a vital part in controlling the world's climate . Farmer use most of the new land to grow soya beans ,Which they export to other parts of the world to be used as animal food . Millions of chickens in western European countries are fed on south American soya beans .Soya beans turned into food for human consumption. Other areas of the rainforest are cleaned by rancher who use the land for their cattle ,by loggers who sell the valuable tropical hardwood from the trees they cut down ,or by oil companies who are trying to find more oil. These activities help to improve the economy of the region ,but at the expense of the future of the global environment .
